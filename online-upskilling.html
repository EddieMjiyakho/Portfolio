Online Upskilling

Continuous Learning and Growth

I use SoloLearn on my phone as part of my upskilling routine because it’s convenient and lets me practice coding anywhere. I prefer spending my free time learning instead of scrolling on social media, and I also like that the platform is interactive, user-friendly, and free.

I also learn through FreeCodeCamp since it provides structured, beginner-friendly lessons and practical coding projects. I follow both their website and YouTube channel, which are trusted by many experienced engineers. Being part of their large learning community keeps my learning consistent, enjoyable, and flexible.

I’m also really interested in cybersecurity and often use TryHackMe for Capture the Flag challenges and real-world security labs. These help me understand vulnerabilities and how defenses work. I also watch cybersecurity tutorials on YouTube to stay updated, which has fueled my curiosity to keep improving and applying what I learn in real-world scenarios.
